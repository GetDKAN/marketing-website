DKAN is a powerful, community-driven open data platform that has been at the forefront of data accessibility and transparency for over a decade. As a Drupal module, DKAN leverages Drupal's robust features to create modern, standards-focused, and API-first open data catalogs for a variety of specific use cases. DKAN is proudly maintained by CivicActions, who have been leaders in Open Source solutions for government and nonprofit organizations for 20 years.

Proud members of the Digital Public Goods Alliance

The Digital Public Goods Alliance is a multi-stakeholder UN-endorsed initiative that facilitates the discovery and deployment of open-source technologies to achieve sustainable development goals.

Why Open Data?

Proprietary software can be rigid, costly, and time-consuming, leaving you at the mercy of the vendor’s pricing and one-size-fits-all approach. Open source software like DKAN allows for customized solutions. It is also publicly funded and owned, with no licensing fees or subscription costs, allowing agencies to take back control of the software from the vendor. Unlike proprietary software, which manages your data, DKAN empowers government and nonprofit organizations to reinstate ownership of their data.
